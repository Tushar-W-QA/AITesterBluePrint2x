VWO Test Metrics Strategy

Defining Quality through Data-Driven Insights

1. Core Execution Metrics

MetricFormulaTarget
Execution Progress(Executed / Planned) * 100100%

2. Advanced Quality Indicators

Defect Removal Efficiency (DRE)

Measures the effectiveness of the testing process before release.

Found in QA / (QA + Prod) * 100

Automation Coverage

Percentage of the regression suite that is fully automated.

Target: > 80%

3. Reporting Dashboards

  • QA Health Dashboard: Visualizing real-time pass/fail trends.
  • Defect Trend Heatmap: Identifying high-risk modules with dense bug counts.
